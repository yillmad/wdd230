Company's Spothlights:

Colca's Adventures - Discover Arequipa's Hidden Treasures

Colca's Adventures is your gateway to unforgettable Arequipa's adventures. As a leading tour operator, we specialize in crafting unique experiences that showcase the natural beauty, rich culture, and historical wonders of "The white city". From trekking the Inca Trail to exploring the Amazon Colca Canion, our expert guides will lead you on an unforgettable journey. Join us and immerse yourself in the magic of Arequipa.

IncaCrafts Emporium - Authentic Arquipa Artisans

At IncaCrafts Emporium, we celebrate Arequipa's vibrant culture through exquisite handmade crafts. Our artisans create unique pieces that reflect the traditions and heritage of the city. From colorful textiles and pottery to intricate jewelry, every item tells a story. Explore our online store and bring a piece of Arequipa's artistic heritage into your life.

Arequipa Gourmet Tours - Savor the Flavors of Arequipa

Arequipa Gourmet Tours invites you to embark on a culinary adventure through Peru's gastronomic paradise. Discover the secrets of Peruvian cuisine as you taste your way through Lima's top restaurants, food markets, and street vendors. Our guided food tours offer an authentic and delicious immersion into Peru's culinary world.
